Seven areas of expertise, one goal: safety

Innovative risk-analysis methodologies applied to the process industry, the environment and human factors.

01 — Industrial safety

Process risk analysis

We identify, assess and manage the risks of industrial plants, supporting regulatory compliance and the prevention of major accidents.

  • Hazard and operability analysis (HAZOP)
  • Consequence and accident-scenario analysis
  • Seveso Directive compliance and safety reports
  • Quantitative risk analysis (QRA)

02 — Workplaces

Operator health and safety

We assess risks in the workplace to protect people's health and ensure compliance with legal obligations.

  • Safety and health risk assessment
  • Industrial hygiene and risk agents
  • Prevention and protection measures
Learn more →
03 — Reliability and maintainability

RAMS studies of plants and systems

We analyse the reliability, availability, maintainability and safety of complex systems to maximise their performance throughout the life cycle.

  • Reliability and availability analysis (RAM)
  • FMEA / FMECA and fault tree analysis (FTA)
  • Optimisation of maintenance strategies
Learn more →
04 — Product safety

Compliance and Product Safety

We take the product from regulatory compliance through to certification and post-sale surveillance, securing its safe access to the market.

  • Regulatory scouting and CE marking
  • Risk assessment (FMEA, ISO 12100) and safety by design
  • Technical file, certification and declaration of conformity
Learn more →
05 — Management systems

Quality, environment, safety and energy

We design and implement management systems compliant with international standards, integrating them into business processes.

  • Quality management systems (ISO 9001)
  • Environmental management systems (ISO 14001)
  • Safety management systems (ISO 45001, UNI 10617/10616)
  • Energy management systems (ISO 50001)
Learn more →
06 — Training

Skills in risk and safety

We deliver specialist training programmes to transfer methodologies and a safety culture to technical staff and management.

  • Courses on risk analysis and methodologies
  • Training on process safety and Seveso
  • Tailored programmes for the organisation
Learn more →
07 — Human factors

Human error and safety culture

We study the role of the human factor in high-risk systems to reduce errors and strengthen the safety culture.

  • Human Reliability Analysis (HRA)
  • Cognitive ergonomics and interface design
  • Safety culture assessment
