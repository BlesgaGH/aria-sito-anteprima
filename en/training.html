Training

Worker information, education and on-site training: tailored programmes to transfer skills and a culture of safety at every corporate level.

What we offer

Training courses

We design and deliver training programmes in the classroom, on company premises and in the field.

Safety courses for all corporate levels

Design and delivery in line with the State-Regions Agreements for:

  • Employers
  • Managers of major-accident hazard plants
  • Supervisors and workers
  • Workers' Safety Representatives (RLS)
  • Emergency response and first-aid personnel

In-depth courses on specific risks and analysis methods

  • Chemical risk and fire risk
  • Explosive atmospheres (ATEX)
  • Machinery Directive and product certification
  • Risk-analysis techniques
  • Safety Integrity Level (SIL)

Product safety

Courses on CE marking, product directives and regulations: risk assessment (ISO 12100), technical file and declaration of conformity, for manufacturers, designers and technical departments.

Environmental training

Waste management, atmospheric emissions and the transport of dangerous goods (ADR Agreement).

Participatory safety

Collaborative programmes for groups of workers that integrate risk perception into assessments.

Programme design and funding

Support in designing tailored programmes and in accessing funding (e.g. Fondimpresa).

Qualified instructors

Trainers holding the qualification and three-yearly refresher requirements of the Italian Interministerial Decree of 6 March 2013 (art. 6(8)(m-bis), Legislative Decree 81/2008), in line with the State-Regions Agreement on training.
