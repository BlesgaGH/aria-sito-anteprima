About us

Safety engineering born from research

ARIA was founded in 2004 and, since 2007, has been a graduate company of I3P – the Innovative Business Incubator of the Politecnico di Torino. For over twenty years we have developed and applied innovative methodologies for industrial and environmental risk analysis, supporting companies and institutions in their most critical decisions.

Our work combines scientific rigour with field experience, continuously fuelled by participation in national and international research projects.

  • ISO 9001 certification issued by DNV since 2005
  • Proprietary risk-analysis methodologies
  • Multidisciplinary approach: process, environment, human factors

What's new

Packaging Vademecum

The operational tool developed by ARIA and ICIM Consulting for compliance in the management of packaging waste across Europe.

Pending a European Regulation harmonising the application of EU Directive 2018/852, the Vademecum describes, through simple and intuitive navigation, the obligations to be met in each European country: packaging labelling requirements, membership of eco-organisations and declarations of the quantities of packaging placed on each market.
