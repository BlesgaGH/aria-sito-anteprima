Cookie Policy

Information about the cookies and similar technologies used by the www.aria.srl website.

Last updated: 9 June 2026

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that the websites visited send to the user's device, where they are stored so that they can be sent back to those same websites on the next visit. Similar technologies (such as web beacons, pixels and local storage) may be used to collect information about the user's behaviour. In the following, for simplicity, all these technologies are referred to by the term "cookies".

2. Cookies used by this website

The www.aria.srl website is a static, mainly informational website and does not use its own profiling cookies or statistical analysis tools (e.g. Google Analytics). Any cookies that may be present fall into the following categories.

a) Technical cookies

These are the cookies necessary for the website to function correctly and for its content to be used. Their use does not require the user's consent.

b) Third-party cookies and services

Some pages embed content and services provided by third parties, which may install their own cookies or involve the transmission of data (for example the IP address) to the respective providers. ARIA has neither access to nor control over third-party cookies; for the relevant terms, please refer to the privacy notices of the providers listed below.

The typefaces (fonts) used for the website's graphic design are hosted directly on the website's own servers (self-hosted): loading them involves no connection to Google's servers and no transmission of the user's IP address to third parties.

3. How to manage or disable cookies

Users can manage their cookie preferences directly from their browser settings, choosing for example to block or delete cookies. Disabling certain cookies may, however, compromise the correct operation of some features of the website (for example the display of the map).
